Ambiguity Factor

Controls how wide the uncertain band is around the calibrated block threshold. When a probe's load time falls inside this band, extra confirmation votes are taken instead of deciding immediately.

0 — no band; decide on the first sample (fastest, noisiest)
0.4 — ±40% of threshold retried (recommended default)
1.0 — always retry all MAX_VOTES (slowest, most accurate)
